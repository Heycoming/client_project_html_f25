Career Exploration

Why a Structured Career Search Matters

  1. Provides clarity and focus: Self-awareness + Market insight + Consistent action + Reflection = a clear direction instead of scattered, random applications.
  2. Embraces iteration, not permanence: Paths evolve as you cycle through Explore → Target → Apply → Interview → Reflect → Adjust.
  3. Builds confidence and momentum: A structured approach breaks the process into manageable steps, making it less overwhelming and more achievable.

Timeline & Planning

Academic Year Snapshot (Reference)

  • Fall: Exploration + networking + early tech internship apps.
  • Winter: Main internship application wave + interview practice.
  • Spring: Late-cycle applications + portfolio refinement + negotiation prep.
  • Summer: Internship execution + reflection + gap filling.

Building a Weekly Routine

  • Example weekly allocation: 2 hrs exploration, 3 hrs applications, 2 hrs networking, 2 hrs portfolio, 1 hr reflection.
